## Scenario Overview

Onboarding funnel: invite link → see what you're joining → sign up with music platform → in the session. Under 2 minutes, effortless.

**Persona:** The Casual — early 30s, scrolling during a break, curious but not invested.
**Entry:** Invite link from group chat (WhatsApp, iMessage, Discord).
**Best outcome:** Account created, platform linked, inside the session. Felt effortless.
