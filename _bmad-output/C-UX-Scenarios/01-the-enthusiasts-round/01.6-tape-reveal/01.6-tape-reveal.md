# 01.6-Tape: Results / Reveal

## Page Metadata

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Scenario** | 01: The Enthusiast's Round |
| **Page Number** | 01.6 |
| **Platform** | Mobile |

## Overview

**Page Purpose:** The emotional payoff — reveal who submitted what and see the comments each song received. No ranking, no scores. Songs displayed in random order. The moment is about identity and connection, not competition.

**Entry Context:** Commenting deadline has passed (or host triggered reveal). User gets a notification ("Reveal is ready!") and taps into the tape, or taps the tape card in session view.

**Exit Action:** Scenario success — the user discovered new music through friends, experienced the reveal. Returns to session view. Loop complete.

## Design Decisions

- **Accordion per song:** Each song is a row. Submitter identity and comments are hidden until the user expands that song. Tap to expand — see who submitted it and what people said.
- **Random order:** Songs are displayed in random (shuffled) order. No ranking, no implied hierarchy. Every song gets equal visual weight.
- **Submitter hidden until expanded:** The reveal moment is per-song. You expand a song and see: who picked it + the comments it received. This preserves the "Jake picked THAT?!" moment for each song individually.
- **Comments visible on expand:** When a song accordion is expanded, all comments for that song are shown alongside the submitter identity.
- **No points shown:** There are no points. The reveal is about identity and conversation, not scores.
- **Tape card (session view):** Compact view shows the song list. Submitter names may be visible in the compact card after the user has visited the reveal page (or always visible — open question).

## Content Needs

- Song listing in random order: song title, artist, album art
- Per-song accordion: submitter name revealed on expand
- Per-song: comments from the commenting phase, shown on expand
- "The Tape" comments (overall reactions) shown at top or bottom
- Tape card (session view): compact view of songs with submitter names

## Driving Forces Addressed

- **Social discovery (15):** THE moment — "Jake picked THAT?!" The core emotional payoff of the entire product.
- **Self-expression:** Seeing what people said about your pick, discovering who resonated with what

## On-Page Interactions (Storyboard Items)

- Accordion expand/collapse per song (reveals submitter + comments)
- Scroll through the full song list with comments
- Navigate back to session view

## Complexity Notes

- Medium complexity — accordion interaction with reveal animation per song
- Random order means no implied hierarchy to manage
- No first-visit vs. revisit state tracking needed — the accordion pattern works the same every time
- The per-song reveal interaction (expand to see submitter + comments) is the signature moment — should feel satisfying
- Animation quality matters here more than anywhere else in the app

## Open Questions

- Should all accordions start collapsed, or should the first one be open as an affordance?
- Should the tape card in session view show submitter names after the user has visited the reveal, or keep them hidden until expanded even in compact view?
- Should "The Tape" overall comments appear at the top before songs or at the bottom after?
