# Business Goals & Objectives

> Strategic goals and measurable objectives for Anonymix

**Document:** Trigger Map - Business Goals
**Created:** 2026-03-24
**Status:** COMPLETE

---

## Vision

**Replace Music League for our friend group with something better — platform-inclusive, ad-free, discovery-focused — and build it well enough that it could work for anyone.**

---

## Business Objectives

### ⭐ PRIMARY GOAL: Active Friend Group Participation (THE ENGINE)
- **Statement:** Get the existing friend group actively participating in regular tape rounds — submitting, commenting, and discovering new music together
- **Metric:** Session participation rate (submissions + comments per tape)
- **Target:** 80%+ participation per tape across 5-15 players
- **Timeline:** Within first 2-3 sessions after launch
- **Impact:** This is the entire reason the product exists. If the friend group doesn't use it actively, nothing else matters. Active participation validates the concept and generates the social proof needed for any future growth.

---

### 🚀 ENGAGEMENT QUALITY (Driven by Active Participation)

**Objective 1: Faster Tape Turnaround**
- **Statement:** Rounds complete faster because the UX removes friction (no ads, native playlists, cleaner flow)
- **Metric:** Average days from tape open to tape close
- **Target:** Tapes complete within configured deadline window (vs. Music League where rounds often stall)
- **Timeline:** Measurable from session 1

**Objective 2: Richer Discussion**
- **Statement:** Comments and chat become a meaningful part of each round, not an afterthought
- **Metric:** Average comments per tape
- **Target:** 3+ comments per tape (indicating people are engaging beyond just submitting)
- **Timeline:** Within first 3 sessions

**Objective 3: Playlist Value**
- **Statement:** Generated playlists become something people actually listen to after the round ends
- **Metric:** Playlist saves/follows on Spotify or YouTube Music
- **Target:** 50%+ of participants save/follow at least some round playlists
- **Timeline:** Within first month

---

### 🌟 ORGANIC GROWTH (If the Engine Works, Growth Follows Naturally)

**Note:** These are stretch outcomes that happen if the core experience is genuinely good. Not optimizing for them — but designing so they're possible.

**Objective 4: Host Expansion**
- **Statement:** Other people in the group start their own sessions because it's easy and fun
- **Metric:** Number of distinct session hosts
- **Target:** 2+ hosts (beyond original creator)
- **Timeline:** Within 2 months of launch

**Objective 5: Word-of-Mouth Spread**
- **Statement:** Players invite friends from outside the original group
- **Metric:** New players from organic invites
- **Target:** Any growth beyond the initial friend group
- **Timeline:** No pressure — happens naturally or not at all

**Objective 6: Sustainability**
- **Statement:** If it grows beyond the friend group, the product sustains itself without ads
- **Metric:** Operational cost coverage (Ko-fi donations or subscription revenue vs. hosting costs)
- **Target:** Break-even on hosting costs if user base grows
- **Timeline:** Only relevant if/when public launch happens

---

## The Flywheel: How Goals Connect

**THE ENGINE (Priority #1):**
- Active friend group participation — people submitting, commenting every round
- Timeline: First 2-3 sessions
- These engaged players validate the concept and create the social energy that makes each tape worthwhile
- They create the flywheel: good rounds → more participation → better playlists → more reasons to come back

**ENGAGEMENT QUALITY (Priority #2):**
- Driven BY active participation — you can't have good discussions or valuable playlists without people showing up
- Faster turnaround, richer comments, playlists worth saving
- Timeline: Ongoing from session 1
- Focus: Making each round feel rewarding beyond just the game mechanic

**ORGANIC GROWTH (Priority #3):**
- Natural outcome of a genuinely good experience
- Others host sessions, players invite friends, product sustains itself
- Timeline: 2+ months, no pressure
- **Key benefit**: The group gets a better music sharing experience; if others benefit too, that's a bonus

## Success Metrics Alignment

### How Trigger Map Connects to Objectives (Properly Prioritized):

**⭐ PRIMARY: Active Participation Drives Everything → Achieves:**
- ✅ **80%+ participation per tape** (THE ENGINE — people show up and engage)
- ✅ Submissions come in before deadline
- ✅ Comments are genuine, not obligated
- ✅ The social loop sustains itself round over round
- **Timeline: First 2-3 sessions**
- **This drives ALL other objectives**

**🚀 SECONDARY: Quality Engagement Follows Participation → Achieves:**
- ✅ Faster tape turnaround (no friction to slow people down)
- ✅ Meaningful comments (people care enough to discuss)
- ✅ Playlists worth saving (good submissions + discovery)
- **Timeline: Ongoing**

**🌟 TERTIARY: Growth Follows Quality → Achieves:**
- ✅ Host expansion (others want to run their own sessions)
- ✅ Organic invites (players bring in friends)
- ✅ Sustainability (covers costs if it scales)
- **Timeline: 2+ months, no pressure**
- **Benefit: Better music sharing for more people**

**The Trigger Map IS the Strategic Foundation — And Prioritization Matters**

The product must make it effortless and rewarding to participate → participation creates quality engagement → quality engagement naturally attracts growth. Design for the engine first. Everything else follows.
